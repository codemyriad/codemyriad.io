Aire Spaces is a small, rapidly growing short-term property management business. They look after a portfolio of properties on behalf of individual owners — the day-to-day of that work is operational and people-driven rather than technical, and the team behind it is not a technical team.

They came to us with a reporting problem: eleven months of property revenue held together inside an interconnected spreadsheet, and a client report assembled and sent out by hand every month. We started there. Each piece of work since has uncovered the next thing worth fixing — and what began as automating a single process has grown into a nascent client portal sitting at the centre of how they operate.

The engagement has grown with the work. When they come to us with what they need next, we shape each piece into a stage with a fixed timeframe and a clear deliverable at the end. We build it, then host and manage it on their behalf. Each stage stands on its own, and what gets built leaves them to run their business rather than their software.
